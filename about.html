About

Misson & Vision

We create thought-provoking films, series, and documentaries that center LGBTQ+ voices and marginalized communities—on screen and behind the scenes. Whether it’s a powerful TEDx talk, an award-winning short, or a bold claymation feature in progress, our projects are made to move people and spark meaningful conversations.

With deep roots in advertising, we also offer branding, marketing, and web design services—helping creators and businesses bring their message to life across platforms. From quirky logos to immersive digital experiences, we craft work that’s as intentional as it is impactful.

At Big Red, we believe in collaboration, transparency, and treating creatives with the respect they deserve. We work with a range of budgets because great ideas—and great storytelling—should be accessible to more than just the well-funded.

If you’re building something with purpose, we’re here to help you make it unforgettable.
