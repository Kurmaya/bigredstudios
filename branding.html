Branding & Marketing

Linda Keller

Linda’s message is deeply spiritual—but her style? Unexpected, joyful, and full of life. We kicked off with a quirky, expressive logo that feels just like her: grounded in faith, but unafraid to have fun. Then came a vibrant prayer card that’s as memorable as her testimony. With a book in the works and a heart set on inspiring worship groups and spiritual communities, Linda’s stepping into the spotlight—and we’re honored to help her shine.

Tanya Everett

When TEDx speaker, actor, and writer Tanya Everett came to us, her voice was strong—we helped turn it into a brand.

We produced her TEDx talk, then crafted a personal logo and identity that reflected her fire and finesse. That evolved into Teatime with Tanya, a live show series spotlighting diverse artists in conversation and performance. From visual design to show strategy and content planning, we shaped a brand that was warm, unfiltered, and unmistakably Tanya.
